Request to Delete Your Brupper Account

If you are a Brupper app user and wish to delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Brupper app
  2. Go to Account Settings > Delete My Account
  3. Confirm your choice. Your account and related data will be permanently deleted.

What will be deleted:

  • Your name, email, and phone number
  • All past order records associated with your account

If you face any issues, please contact us at support@nohung.com.
