Brupper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use the Brupper food delivery app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Delivery Address
  • Order Details
  • Device Information (for crash analytics and app improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your identity using OTP or email login
  • Process your food orders
  • Contact you for order confirmation and delivery
  • Send transactional notifications and updates
  • Improve user experience and customer support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Delivery partners – to deliver your order (name, contact, and address)
  • Service providers – for app hosting, analytics, and notifications

4. Data Security

Your data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored using industry-standard protections. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

5. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly from the app or by visiting:

https://shamiluna.github.io/policy/delete-account.html

Upon deletion:

  • Your personal data will be permanently removed
  • Order history will be deleted unless retention is required by law

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or correct your personal information
  • Request account and data deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time (which may limit app functionality)

7. Children’s Privacy

The Brupper app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
